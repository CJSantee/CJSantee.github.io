About Me

Hey! I'm Colin, a creative entrepreneur with a passion for music, photo, and video. I'm a classically trained guitarist now working as a guitar instructor and musician. I started playing guitar in 2008 and have since taught myself piano, ukulele, drums, and bass guitar. I fell in love with the art of photography and cinematography shortly after I first handled a DSLR camera in 2013. In addition to documenting my own travels, I've use my photo and video skills to cover events for International Students Incoporated. Now I'm a Software Engineering student at Lipscomb University in Nashville, TN.
